Recipe Helper App

My wife and I have something like 5 cookbooks, a box of recipe cards, and a bunch of Publix special recipes. It turns it a long exercise to plan out meals and even longer exercise to find that recipe again in all those different sources when it comes time to make it. The idea behind the recipe helper app is to simplify that process and centralize those sources into a single, searchable repository.

Games Catalog

I love video games, and part of that love is that fact that I have a ton of games laying around that I haven't finished or heck that I haven't even started. The Games Catalog app is a repository, review and comment system, and game completion status tracker. This project is much more of a place to test / learn new technologies, so there may several different version of a specific application piece.
