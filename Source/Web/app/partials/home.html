Let's Eat Out

So I have kids! Lots of them, well not lots but there are more of them than my wife and I. And they are bottomless pits when they decide they are hungry and want to eat. So it can be expensive going out to dinner. So I decided to figure out all the places near me that have offers for eating out with kids. This website displays which restaurant has a meal deal for any given day of the week.
